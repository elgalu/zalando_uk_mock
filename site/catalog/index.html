        Nike

        One of the most famous brands in the world, Nike is revolutionary in its approach to sportswear design. Founded in 1964, Nike took its name from the Greek goddess of victory, and it has been empowering its fans ever since.


        The Nike sports gods


        If you want to get your pulse racing and your body working, Nike is certainly on side. From yoga pants and sweatshirts, to tracksuits and running shorts, Nike is responsible for some of the latest designs and must-have items. Ensure your little ones are kitted out for sports day with a pair of Nike kid's classic trainers but bring your 'A' game to your own workouts with some Nike women's running tights. However you're exercising, Nike will keep you in check and in fashion.


        Nike on the catwalk


        Ever since sportswear took to the catwalk, we have been looking to Nike for some key fashion pieces. Nike men's sports shirts have always been popular with jogging bottoms on a lazy Sunday, but layer one with a sweatshirt and an oversized rain coat to bring them to another level. From varsity t-shirts to sheer panelled leggings, women's sports gear has seen a fashion overhaul over the past year. Dress up a retro gym vest with a pencil skirt for a grown up sports luxe look, or inject your summer wardrobe with some editorial style by teaming some rainbow running shoes with your favourite summer dress.
